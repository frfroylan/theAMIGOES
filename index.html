About Us

Mission

NOAH

Being a business professional and a father to an 8 year old girl are the highest points of his life. Being able to raise and prepare her for what she would come to experience in her life has been the most fulfilling duty he has had, passing down tradition, belief systems, and concepts on right and wrong... Even with all that he has been able to do and provide for her, she had asked him a simple question that he couldn't answer. Who was he before she was born? We love that he taught her to become curious, to try and discover the meaning of things she doesn't understand. But this question caught him completely off guard. He tried to answer it but in the process he had to come to realize a few things himself. His life. This gift, he has yet to even experience the cusp of what makes it beautiful. He has taken a simplified path. Rushing to get by. Running in this rat race, that sometime along the way her forgot to sit back and enjoy it. Take a chance, forget the rules, be UNIQUE! We all have this point of self discovery in our lifes, it may come as a significant other, an accident, or maybe a simple bio you read online. Regardless we all have a chance to write our own stories...the real question is, how many pages is yours going to be? For him...he wants his legacy to be of a man who took a chance and closed his eyes and reopened them with no regrets.
